About Us

Tourism department of Kerala Government


Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife.
Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
Chief minister: Pinarayi Vijayan
Population: 3.46 crores (2018)
Governor: Arif Mohammad Khan
Founded: 1 November 1956

The Department of Tourism is a major government ministry under the Kerala Government that regulates and supervises tourism in Kerala.
Founded: 1995, Thiruvananthapuram
Child agencies: KTDC; TRKL
Agency executives: Dr. Venu V. IAS, Principal Secretary; Sri. U. V. Jose IAS, Director
Jurisdiction: Kerala
